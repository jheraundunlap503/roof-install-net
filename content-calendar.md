## Ongoing — Every Week

- 1 "People Also Ask" FAQ article per week (fast-ranking, low competition)
- 1 Reddit-sourced pain point article per week (real homeowner language)
- Monitor Google Search Console weekly — request indexing on new pages
- Update any article older than 6 months with fresh data

---

## Content Priority If Resources Are Limited

If you can only produce limited content in a given week, prioritize in this order:
1. Insurance claim content (highest buyer intent, most shared)
2. Cost and ROI content (decision stage, drives estimator tool traffic)
3. Scam protection content (highest share rate, neighborhood Facebook groups)
4. Material comparison content (tile vs shingle is evergreen)
5. Service pages (East Valley cities before national)
6. FAQ content (fastest to rank, lowest effort)
